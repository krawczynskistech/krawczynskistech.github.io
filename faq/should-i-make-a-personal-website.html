Should I make a personal website?

Many people have a hard time deciding about making a personal website. You’re not alone, and we are here to help you make the decision.

Social media / Boosting your recognizability

A website is good if you want to get more clients or boost your recognizability. If you want to get more followers on social media ,and be generally better recognizable , then a website might be the right thing for you.

Applying for a job - Addition to CV / Resume

A website is a great idea if you are applying for a Job. It can be a really good addition to your CV/Resume. It will make a unique first impression for the employer, and will increase your chances of landing your Job. You will stand out among the other people that only have a CV/Resume.

For buisnesses

If you have a business, a website is a must-have. It’s really good for catching clients. It’s like a worldwide ad that will interest some customers, if it’s properly done.

What if i don't have a buisness/social media/ i'm not applying for a job

If you don’t have a business, don’t want to gain clients or recognizability, don’t want to stand out while applying to a Job, or don’t want to boost your social media, then a website will probably change nothing for you, so I don’t recommend buying it.
